About Tony's Dockside Services

WELCOME TO 20TH STREET AUTO

Tony's Dockside Services has been Marco Island's go-to boat repair shop since 1987. Located at 960 Chalmer Dr, we are an authorized Mercury and Yamaha service center and an authorized Power-Pole dealer — serving boat owners across Marco Island, Isles of Capri, and the surrounding waterways.

Whether your outboard needs a tune-up, your hull needs repair, or you're looking to add a Power-Pole anchoring system, our experienced team handles it with precision. We've kept the boats of Marco Island running for nearly four decades — and we stand behind every repair we make.
